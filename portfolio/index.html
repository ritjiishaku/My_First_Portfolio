Introduction

Hi, I'm Ritji Ishaku—a UX/UI Designer and Front-End Web Engineer with a background in healthcare as a registered nurse. My journey into UX design was driven by my passion for creating impactful digital solutions that connect with users. While working as a nurse, I noticed a gap in how many digital healthcare products failed to address user needs effectively, leading me to pivot my career toward designing solutions that truly make a difference.

With over a year of experience, I specialize in crafting user-centered interfaces for healthcare digital products and early-stage startups. I focus on helping startups boost conversions, enhance user engagement, and reduce bounce rates through intuitive, clean, and user-friendly designs. My expertise extends to web and mobile applications, blending my knowledge of healthcare, front-end development, and design to deliver seamless digital experiences.

Currently, I’m working on a telemedicine and AI healthcare platform aligned with SDG Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being in Africa, building both mobile and web applications to address critical healthcare challenges. My work combines innovation and empathy, ensuring accessible and meaningful solutions for diverse users.

When I’m not designing, you can find me exploring cutting-edge technologies like AI/ML and generative AI or brainstorming ways to align digital solutions with global challenges. My mission is to create experiences that drive both business growth and user satisfaction.

Let’s connect and create something impactful together!
